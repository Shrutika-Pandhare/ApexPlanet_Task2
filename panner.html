Panner Butter Masala

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp fresh cream
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil and butter in a pan. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until raw smell goes away.
  3. Stir in tomato puree. Cook until the oil separates.
  4. Add turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Add paneer cubes and mix gently. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Add fresh cream, mix well, and cook for another 2 minutes.
  7. Garnish with coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.
