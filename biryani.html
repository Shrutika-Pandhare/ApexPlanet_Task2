Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cleaned and cut into pieces
  • 2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • A few whole spices (bay leaf, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon)
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves, chopped
  • 4 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Steps

  1. Heat oil/ghee in a pot. Add whole spices and sauté for a few seconds.
  2. Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until fragrant.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft. Then add turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, and salt.
  5. Add chicken pieces and cook until they turn white. Add yogurt, mix well, and cook for 10 minutes.
  6. Add chopped coriander and mint leaves. Layer soaked rice over the chicken evenly.
  7. Pour enough water to cook the rice (about 3.5 to 4 cups). Cover and cook on low heat until rice is done and water is absorbed.
  8. Let it rest for 10 minutes, then gently fluff and serve hot with raita or salad.
