Vegetable Curry

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chopped vegetables (carrot, beans, peas)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, pureed
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions until golden brown.
  2. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  3. Mix in turmeric, garam masala, and salt. Cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add chopped vegetables and 1/2 cup water.
  5. Cover and cook until vegetables are soft.
  6. Garnish with coriander and serve hot with rice or roti.
