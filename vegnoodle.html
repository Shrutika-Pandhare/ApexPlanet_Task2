Veg Garlic Noodles

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked noodles
  • 1 tbsp garlic, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers (any color)
  • 1/2 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/4 cup sliced carrots
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce (optional)
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spring onions for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a wok or pan. Add chopped garlic and sauté until golden.
  2. Add carrots, cabbage, and bell peppers. Stir-fry on high heat for 2–3 minutes.
  3. Add soy sauce, chili sauce (if using), vinegar, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  4. Add cooked noodles and toss everything together on high heat.
  5. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.
