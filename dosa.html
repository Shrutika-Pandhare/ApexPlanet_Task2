Masala Dosa

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dosa batter (fermented rice and urad dal mixture)
  • 2 boiled potatoes, mashed
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • A few curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add curry leaves, green chili, and sliced onions. Sauté until golden.
  3. Mix in turmeric and salt. Add mashed potatoes and mix well.
  4. Cook for 2–3 minutes, stirring occasionally. Set aside.
  5. Heat a non-stick pan or dosa tawa. Pour a ladle of dosa batter and spread it in a circle.
  6. Drizzle a few drops of oil around the edges and cook until the bottom is golden and crisp.
  7. Place some potato masala in the center. Fold the dosa and serve hot with chutney and sambar.
