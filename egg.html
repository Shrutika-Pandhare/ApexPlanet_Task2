Egg Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cooled)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup chopped spring onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/4 cup chopped capsicum
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan or wok. Crack in the eggs and scramble them. Cook until just set, then push aside.
  2. Add chopped vegetables and stir-fry on high heat for 2â€“3 minutes.
  3. Add the cooked rice and mix everything together.
  4. Add soy sauce, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Toss well on high flame.
  5. Add spring onions, give a final mix, and serve hot.
