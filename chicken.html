VFried Chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken pieces (bone-in or boneless)
  • 1 cup buttermilk (or 1/2 cup yogurt + 1/2 cup water)
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder (or minced garlic)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cornflour (optional, for extra crispiness)
  • Oil for deep frying

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with buttermilk, chili powder, pepper, garlic, and salt. Rest for 1 hour or overnight in the fridge.
  2. In a bowl, mix all-purpose flour and cornflour with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan or kadai for frying.
  4. Remove chicken from marinade, coat well in flour mixture, and shake off excess.
  5. Fry in hot oil on medium heat until golden brown and cooked through (about 8â€“10 minutes depending on size).
  6. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with ketchup or dip of choice.
