Rice Cracker

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice (cooled and slightly mashed)
  • 2 tbsp rice flour
  • 1/2 tsp sesame seeds (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying or baking
  • Water as needed (if shaping the dough)

Steps

  1. Mix cooked rice, rice flour, sesame seeds, and salt into a dough. Add a little water if needed.
  2. Roll small portions into thin discs or flatten with hands.
  3. For frying: Heat oil and fry until golden and crispy. Drain on paper towels.
  4. For baking: Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Place shaped crackers on a tray and bake for 15–20 minutes or until crisp, flipping halfway.
  5. Cool and store in an airtight container. Serve as a snack.
