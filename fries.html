Sweet Potato Fries

Ingredients

  • 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into fries
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. In a large bowl, toss sweet potato fries with olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Arrange fries in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Bake for 20-25 minutes, turning halfway through, until crispy and golden.
  5. Serve hot with your favorite dip.
