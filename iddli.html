Iddli

Ingredients

  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 2 cups idli rice (or parboiled rice)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for greasing the idli molds

Steps

  1. Wash and soak urad dal and idli rice separately for 4–6 hours.
  2. Grind urad dal into a smooth batter and rice into a slightly coarse batter. Mix both together.
  3. Cover and ferment the batter overnight or for 8–10 hours until it rises.
  4. Add salt and gently mix the batter. Grease idli molds with oil.
  5. Pour batter into the molds and steam for 10–12 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Let them cool slightly, then remove and serve hot with chutney and sambar.
