Clam Noodles

Ingredients

  • 200g clams (cleaned)
  • 2 cups cooked noodles
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp fish sauce (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander or spring onion for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add garlic and onions, sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add clams and cook for 2–3 minutes until they open up.
  3. Add soy sauce, fish sauce (if using), and chili flakes. Stir well.
  4. Add the cooked noodles and toss everything together.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with fresh herbs and serve hot.
